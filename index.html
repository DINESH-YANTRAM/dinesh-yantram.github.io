About Yantram Medtech

From a single problem – missed early warning signs – to a full ecosystem for continuous, intelligent care.

Yantram Medtech Pvt Ltd is a health-tech startup building an integrated ecosystem of wearable medical devices, AI-driven patient management software, and mobile applications that connect patients, nurses, doctors and systems on one unified platform.

We started with a simple but vital question: “How many complications in newborns and adults could be prevented if we had continuous, structured data at the right time?”

Our team has taken multiple products from concept to clinical pilots and commercialization, secured competitive grants in India and the UK, and built an interoperable hospital platform designed for the realities of high-volume, resource-constrained settings.

“We want early detection and intelligent triage to be the default, not the exception – from a village PHC to a tertiary hospital.”

Our Journey

  • 2019–2020 Identified gaps in neonatal hypothermia and fever monitoring in low-resource environments.
  • 2021–2022 Built early prototypes of continuous temperature wearables; ran pilots with clinicians and NGOs.
  • 2023 Expanded into a full patient management and triage platform using global standards (SNOMED CT, LOINC, FHIR).
  • 2024 Commercial launch of neonatal temperature monitoring device (NV-Core) and deployment in ambulances and community programs.
  • 2025 & beyond Scaling multi-vital patches, breast-health thermal screening concepts, and AI-enabled decision support across hospitals and public health systems.

A unified ecosystem of devices, software and apps

Devices

NV-Core – Neonatal Temperature Monitor

A medical-grade wearable sensor that continuously tracks a baby’s body temperature and alerts caregivers to hypothermia or fever, enabling timely Kangaroo Mother Care (KMC) or hospital referral.

  • Designed for homes, ambulances and neonatal units
  • Comfortable patch-based form factor
  • Bluetooth connectivity to mobile apps

VitalZ – Multi-vital Monitoring Patch (in development)

A compact wearable patch designed to continuously track key vitals such as heart rate, respiratory rate, SpO₂ and temperature, with AI algorithms to flag early deterioration.

  • Built for wards, step-down units and home monitoring
  • Integrates with Yantram’s patient management platform
  • Enables nurse-light, AI-assisted monitoring

Breast Health & KMC Innovations (pipeline)

Exploration of thermal-sensor-based breast health screening concepts and KMC-supporting wearables such as a Kangaroo Mother Care sling with integrated proximity and safety sensors, to support women’s health and newborn care in low-resource settings.

Software & Mobile Apps

Yantram Patient Management Platform (HIMS + CDSS)

A cloud-based, modular platform that brings triage, vitals, clinical notes, tasks and alerts into one place.

  • Built using SNOMED CT and LOINC for structured data
  • AI-based triage aligned with AIIMS emergency protocol
  • Supports patient transfers, bed management and OT workflows
  • Designed for high patient volumes with low IT overhead

Mobile apps for nurses, doctors & families

Simple, intuitive mobile apps to keep everyone in sync across the care journey.

  • Nurse app for vitals capture, device pairing and alerts
  • Doctor app for live patient lists, trends and decision support
  • Parent / caregiver views for safe home monitoring in neonatal programs
  • Designed to be multilingual and easy to adopt
